⬡ Radar Chart — Field Setup

The radar chart reads from Tableau's Marks card.
Follow these steps to connect your data.

1
Add your dimension to the Marks card
Drag your grouping field (e.g. Spatial Unit, Region) to the Detail shelf on the left. This becomes the color / series on the radar.
2
Add your measure fields as spokes
Drag each measure (e.g. Cultural, Education, Finance, Health, Retail) also to Detail. Each measure becomes one spoke on the radar.
3
Click "Load Data" below
The extension reads all fields from the worksheet and shows a configuration screen where you assign which field is the dimension and which are spokes.

Configure Fields

Choose which field is your color/group dimension and which fields become spokes on the radar.

Tip: numeric/measure fields make the best spokes. String/dimension fields work best as the group.
